[h3] Tools Compatibility: [/h3]
The Building Menu tools dictionary now include a wide range of items from popular mods like "Tools of the Trade," "[Reworked] MWPWeapons," "Authentic Z," and "Kwin's Melee Weapon Pack." This means tools like hammers, sledgehammers, screwdrivers, and more from these mods can now be utilized within the BM.
